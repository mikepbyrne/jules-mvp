Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Phone number, age verification data, payment information

Conversation Data: Text messages exchanged with Jules for service delivery and improvement

Usage Data: Session timestamps, message frequency, interaction patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver personalized AI companion services
  • Maintain conversation context and memory
  • Detect crisis situations for safety
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve service quality

3. Data Security

All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. Personal information is encrypted using industry-standard encryption (Fernet encryption). PII is redacted from system logs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Limited sharing only with:

  • Veriff: Age verification processing
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, SMS delivery (Bandwidth API)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to prevent harm

5. Crisis Reporting (CA SB 243)

When crisis situations are detected, we create anonymous reports for regulatory compliance. These reports do not include personally identifiable information.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (30-day processing)
  • Opt-Out: Text STOP to immediately stop receiving messages
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data

7. Data Retention

Active conversations: Retained while subscription is active

After cancellation: Deleted within 30 days unless required for legal compliance

Anonymous crisis reports: Retained for regulatory reporting (CA SB 243)

8. Children's Privacy

Jules is only available to users 18 and older. We verify age before providing service access.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. Users will be notified of material changes.

11. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us through GitHub Issues or your account support channel.
