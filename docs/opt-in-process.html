SMS Opt-In Process

TCPA Compliance Documentation - Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

Jules uses SMS text messaging to deliver AI companion services. This document explains our opt-in process and your rights under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

Opt-In Process

Step 1: Age Verification

Before receiving SMS access, you must:

  • Verify you are 18 years or older
  • Complete identity verification through Veriff (secure third-party service)
  • Process typically takes 2-3 minutes

Step 2: Express Consent

You will be asked to provide express written consent to receive:

  • Automated text messages from Jules AI companion
  • Service notifications and updates
  • Account and billing information

You must actively agree to receive messages. Pre-checked boxes or passive agreements do not constitute valid consent.

Step 3: Phone Number Assignment

After consent, you will receive:

  • A dedicated Jules phone number for your conversations
  • A welcome message confirming opt-in
  • Instructions on how to use the service

Message Frequency

  • Conversational Messages: Variable based on your interaction (you control the conversation)
  • Service Messages: Occasional (account updates, billing reminders)
  • AI Disclosure Reminders: Every 3 hours during active conversations (NY law requirement)

Messaging Hours (TCPA Compliance)

Jules will only send you messages between:

  • 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM in your local timezone
  • Messages sent outside these hours will be queued for delivery the next business day
  • Exception: Crisis support messages may be sent at any time for safety

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your plan.

Opt-Out (STOP)

You can opt-out at any time by texting STOP to Jules.

When you text STOP:

  • You will immediately stop receiving messages
  • Your phone number is added to our suppression list within seconds
  • You will receive one final confirmation message
  • Your subscription will be cancelled (no further billing)

Alternative opt-out methods:

  • Cancel through your account settings
  • Contact support through GitHub Issues

Help & Support

Text HELP to Jules for assistance, or contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues in the Jules repository
  • Account support portal

Your TCPA Rights

  • You have the right to revoke consent at any time
  • Consent is specific to this service (not shared with third parties for marketing)
  • You will not be penalized for refusing to provide consent
  • Opt-out requests are processed immediately

Carrier Information

Supported Carriers: All major US carriers (AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, etc.)

SMS Provider: Bandwidth API (regulated carrier)

If you experience delivery issues, contact your carrier to ensure SMS is enabled on your plan.

Record Keeping

We maintain records of:

  • Date and time of opt-in consent
  • IP address and device information
  • Opt-out requests and processing timestamps
  • Age verification completion

These records are maintained for TCPA compliance and dispute resolution.

Automated Messages Disclosure

Jules uses automated text messaging technology powered by AI. By opting in, you consent to receive:

  • AI-generated conversational responses
  • Automated service notifications
  • System-generated alerts (crisis detection, AI disclosure reminders)

Privacy

Your phone number and message content are handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Questions?

For questions about the opt-in process or your consent, contact us through GitHub Issues or your account support portal.
