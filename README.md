## Safety & Compliance

Jules is built with your safety as the top priority:

### Crisis Detection
If Jules detects you may be in crisis, you'll immediately receive information for the **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** - a free, confidential service available 24/7.

All users must verify they are 18+ through a secure third-party service (Veriff) before accessing Jules.

### Privacy Protection
- End-to-end encryption for all messages
- No sharing of personal information
- Anonymous crisis reporting for safety (CA SB 243 compliance)

Jules will remind you periodically that you're talking to an AI companion, not a human (required by NY AI Companion Law).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Jules a real person?**
No. Jules is an AI companion powered by advanced language models. Jules will remind you of this periodically to maintain transparency.

**Can Jules help me in a crisis?**
Jules can detect crisis situations and will immediately provide the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline number. However, Jules is not a substitute for emergency services. If you're in immediate danger, call 911.

**How does Jules remember our conversations?**
Jules uses secure memory systems to maintain context across conversations, making interactions feel more natural and personalized over time.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All conversations are encrypted, and personal information is never shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

**Can I use Jules if I'm under 18?**
No. Jules requires age verification and is only available to users 18 and older (CA SB 243 compliance).

**What hours can I text Jules?**
Jules is available 24/7, but will only send you messages between 8 AM - 9 PM in your local timezone (TCPA compliance).

**How do I cancel?**
Text STOP to Jules or cancel through your account settings anytime.
