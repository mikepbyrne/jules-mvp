Jules Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 29, 2025

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

TL;DR: We only collect what's needed for meal planning (your phone number, household info, recipes). We never share your data. You can delete everything anytime by replying STOP.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Phone Number: Required for SMS messaging
  • Household Information: Names, ages, and dietary restrictions of household members (provided during onboarding)
  • Recipes: Recipe names, ingredients, instructions you share with Jules
  • Meal Plans: Weekly meal plans you create
  • Conversation History: Your text messages with Jules (last 30 messages kept)

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Timestamps: When messages are sent/received
  • Opt-in/Opt-out Events: When you subscribe or unsubscribe
  • Message Status: Delivery and read status from Twilio

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for providing the Jules meal planning service:

  • Sending you personalized meal suggestions based on dietary restrictions
  • Generating shopping lists from your meal plans
  • Remembering your recipes and household preferences
  • Providing conversational AI assistance
  • Improving the accuracy of meal recommendations

3. How We Store Your Information

Storage: Your data is stored in secure JSON files on our server.

Location: United States

Encryption: Data at rest is stored securely; data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS

Retention: We keep your data as long as you're opted-in. After opt-out, data is deleted within 30 days.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Period.

Limited exceptions (required for service operation):

  • Twilio: SMS messaging provider (phone number only, required for delivery)
  • Anthropic: AI service provider (conversation text only, no phone numbers or personal identifiers)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right To:

  • Access Your Data: Text "PROFILE" to see your household information
  • Correct Your Data: Update household info or dietary restrictions anytime
  • Delete Your Data: Reply "STOP" to opt-out and have all data deleted within 30 days
  • Export Your Data: Contact us for a copy of your recipes and household data
  • Pause Messages: Reply "PAUSE" to temporarily stop messages without deleting data

6. SMS Messaging Specific Terms

  • Consent Required: We only send messages to users who have explicitly opted-in
  • Message Frequency: Varies by usage (typically 10-50 messages/week)
  • Message Costs: Standard message and data rates apply
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance
  • Supported Carriers: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, and most major carriers

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Jules is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Household member information (including children's names, ages, dietary restrictions) is provided by adults and used only for meal planning purposes.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes:

  • We will notify active users via SMS
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price (no discrimination)

11. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access, rectify, or erase your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
